This site was designed and developed by the Fall 2020 Interactive 2 class at California College of the Arts.

Interactive 2 reflects on the conception of electronic tools and their social context. Investigation therefore requires us to develop technical skills to build inventive interactive works as a response. Our primary focus however will be on research, with conceptual and critical thinking being a key rather than purely technical development.
